Residual Modulation Experiment

Question: Is the modulated value agent's failure due to the gate (gradient starvation) or the training method (self-play), or both?

Mechanism hypothesis: The gate attenuates gradients for delta by ~0.4×, and self-play produces small residuals (V_base is already good at self-play). Together, these starve the modulation network of learning signal. Removing the gate and using population training should restore gradient flow.
